Distance Reporter tracks your distance traveled using GPS and announces your progress via text-to-speech at configurable intervals.

Features:
• GPS-based distance tracking
• Audible distance announcements at configurable intervals
• Support for kilometers, miles, and nautical miles
• Calendar view showing daily distance history
• Pause and mute controls
• Real-time speed display
• Runs as a foreground service for reliable tracking
• No Google Play Services required
• No ads, no tracking, no internet permission required

Perfect for walkers, runners, cyclists, and anyone who wants to track their daily movement without looking at their phone.
